Real Estate Investment Banking

Senior, Mezzanine & Equity Financing, Brokerage

Strength & Intelligence

With experience of over forty years and several billion dollars in closings, Griffin’s team is in a unique position to advise clients effectively.

Griffin is a full service Real Estate Investment Banking firm, headquartered in Naples, Florida. Griffin has positioned itself to successfully advise clients regarding equity, preferred equity, JV, mezzanine and senior financing, maximizing client objectives. Griffin’s capital platform is the solution for construction and or long-term financing for apartments, retail, industrial, office, hospitality (limited service, full service, resort), seniors housing, high-rise, mid-rise and low-rise condominium projects, apartment-to-condominium conversions, golf course communities, trophy properties, regional malls, power centers, net leased properties, as well as other property types. Griffin is the capital stack funding solution for joint ventures, acquisitions and exit strategies, maximizing terms for ground up, value add, and heavy lift transactions.

Griffin executes complete existing portfolio refinances, partner buyouts, recapitalizations as well as debt portfolio sales.
